About Trip Up Cumilla

Trip Up Cumilla organises domestic tours across Bangladesh — family outings, student group trips, and private packages. We arrange transport, guides, food options and comfortable stays. Destinations include Cox's Bazar, Bandarban, Rangamati, Sajek, Tangwar haor, Shitakundu, Sundarban, Sylhet, Jaflong, and Sreemangal.
